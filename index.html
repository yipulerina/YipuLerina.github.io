How I got into Data Analysis. My Crazy Story!

It all started with a simple mistake: trying to skip an advertisement on YouTube. As I frantically clicked the "Skip Ad" button, I found myself captivated by the video that was playing. It was about data analysis, a topic that had never crossed my mind before. But something about the way the presenter talked about uncovering insights from large amounts of data intrigued me.
Three hours later, I was still on YouTube, watching video after video about data analysis. That's when I realized I had stumbled onto something special. Without even realizing it, I had found my passion.
That night, I enrolled in a 6-month Professional Google Data Analytics Course. The course was challenging, but it was also incredibly rewarding. I learned how to use tools like 'Biq Query',Tableau and 'R' to analyze data and create meaningful reports. But after six months, I realized that I wanted to learn even more.
That's when I decided to enroll in a 1-year Full Stack Data Science Bootcamp.
It's been more than four months now since I enrolled in the bootcamp, I still can't believe how far I've come. My accidental foray into data analysis has turned into a full-fledged career, and I couldn't be happier. Looking back on my journey, I realize that I'm not the same person I was before. I'm more confident, more skilled, and more excited about what the future holds.
